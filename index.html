About Us

BlockCreds is a one step solution to verify credentials.

Lately employee verification has been a huge issue and we are trying to solve this problem. With remote work taking over it will become huge challenge to verify individual credentials.

BlockCreds is involved in solving huge problem of verification and validation. Once verified it will stay on chain and users can share simple url to showcase their credentials.

Issuer invites recipient to receive a blockchain credential. Recipient accepts the invite sending their blockchain address. Issuer hashes credential into blockchain. Issuer sends recipient a blockchain credential. Recipient sends credential to verifier.

Employee's digital records are registered on a blockchain, cryptographically signed, tamper-proof, and shareable. The goal is to enable a wave of innovation that gives individuals the capacity to possess and share their own official records.

  • Easy to verify
  • Easy to share
  • 100% trusted and tamper proof
